Birds of a Feather (or BoF) sessions allow ad-hoc groups to meet and discuss issues that may have a more narrow interest than regular conference sessions, or get together as a group of like-minded individuals to talk about common problems. Some BoFs are run as moderated discussions. Some are shared work sessions where everyone brings their computer and helps each other out. Others are more presentation or panel-discussion style. It's really up to whoever is planning the BoF to choose the topic and the format.

Birds of a Feather (BoF) rooms are open all day from Tuesday, 23rd August at 8am through Friday, 26th at 5pm.

BoF sessions can be scheduled electronically. To schedule a BoF, or to see what BoFs have already been scheduled, visit the DrupalCon website.
